Emergency water and food supplies

Keep at least a three-day supply of water (one gallon per person per day) and non-perishable food items like canned goods, granola bars, and dried fruits.


Emergency communication plan

Establish a communication plan with family members and emergency contacts. Include phone numbers, email addresses, and a designated meeting place.


Important documents

Store copies of important documents such as identification, insurance policies, medical records, and bank account information in a waterproof container.
